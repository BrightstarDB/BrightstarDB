BrightstarDB Privacy Policy

Information we collect about you

When you interact with BrightstarDB Limited, we sometimes receive or collect personal information about you.

For example, if you sign up for our customer portal to receive a trial license or if you purchase licenses from us, you will tell us who you are and how we can contact you.

When you use this site we may use your IP address and cookies to provide certain functionality and to better understand how our site is being used as described in our Cookie Policy.

What We Use Your Information For

We will use the information you provide for a number of purposes including the following:

  • To contact you. For example we may email you to inform you of updates to our software or to the services we provide you. We will only contact you with bulk emails if you opt-in to receive such email and you may opt out of these mailings at any time.
  • To provide you with services. For example to provide you with direct email or telephone support.

Confidentiality

We will keep your personal information cofidential and we will not share that information with third parties without first asking your permission, except where disclosure is required by law (for example to government bodies and law enforcement agencies).

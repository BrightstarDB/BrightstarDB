BrightstarDB Cookie Policy

To improve the user experience when using our site we sometimes place small data files on your device (computer, smart phone, tablet, etc). These are known as cookies. Cookies help improve the experience by measuring how our website is used so we can prioritise improvements. You can manage or delete these files as you wish. To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit http://www.aboutcookies.org/. Below you can find more information about how and where we use cookies.

Measuring Website Usage

We use Google Analytics cookies to collect information about how visitors use this site and when issues are encountered (such as broken links). Google Analytics stores information about the pages you visit, how long you are on the site, how you got here and what you click on. We do not store your personal information so this information cannot be used to identify who you are. Google provides more details on the Google Privacy Policy. Google also provides a browser add-on that allows you to opt out of Google Analytics across all websites.
